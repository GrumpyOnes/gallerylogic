GalleryLogic Support

Technical support · App: GalleryLogic (com.artshot.app)

GalleryLogic is a museum and gallery photography app for iPhone and iPad. If you need help with the app, start with the topics below or contact us directly.

Email support himkominn@gmail.com

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. You can also use Settings → Feedback in the app to pre-fill diagnostic info.

Getting started

  • Create a Collection, then capture artworks from the Camera tab.
  • Short-press the shutter for the default mode; long-press for a one-shot reverse mode.
  • Browse your catalog in three levels: Collection → Artwork → Photo.
  • Optional permissions (photos, microphone, location) can be changed anytime in iOS Settings.

Purchases & Pro

  • Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple. We do not receive your card details.
  • If Pro does not unlock after purchase, open Settings → Restore Purchase.
  • For billing issues, you may also review subscriptions and purchases in your Apple ID settings.

Photos & data

  • Your gallery is stored on your device. Uninstalling the app removes app-private data.
  • If you enabled album sync, copies saved to the system Photos library remain there.
  • Settings → Clean up orphaned records removes catalog entries whose linked system photo was deleted. It does not delete your original files in Photos.

Privacy

GalleryLogic works primarily on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details on permissions, local storage, and purchases.

快速上手

  • 先创建作品集，在「拍摄」中拍摄作品。
  • 短按快门为默认模式；长按为单次反向模式。
  • 图库按 作品集 → 作品 → 照片 三级浏览。
  • 相册、麦克风、位置等权限可在 iOS 设置中随时调整。

购买与 Pro

  • Pro 为一次性应用内购买，由 Apple 处理付款。
  • 购买后未解锁，请在设置 → 恢复购买中重试。
  • 账单相关问题也可在 Apple ID 的购买记录中查看。

照片与数据

  • 图库数据保存在您的设备上；卸载应用会清除应用内私有数据。
  • 若已开启相册同步，保存到系统相册的副本仍保留在相册中。
  • 设置 → 清理孤立记录仅移除系统相册中已不存在的目录条目，不会删除相册原文件。

Your visits, neatly filed

Turn a folder of museum snapshots into visit records you can actually browse. Group shots by exhibition or trip, add dates and companions, and return weeks later without digging through the camera roll.

把一次看展里随手拍下的照片，整理成清晰可查的参观记录。按展览或行程分组，补上日期与同行者，数周后再想回味，不必在相册里翻找。

Speaks your language, fits your lighting

Switch between English, Simplified or Traditional Chinese, and Japanese. Light and dark themes adapt to bright gallery halls or late-night scrolling on the train ride home.

界面提供英语、简体中文、繁体中文与日语，并可在浅色与深色主题间切换。明亮的展厅与回程路上的夜间浏览，都能用更顺眼的方式查看记录。

One artwork, many angles

Keep the full painting, a close-up of brushwork, and the wall label together under one entry. Title, artist, year, and location stay attached so the story behind each piece does not fade after you leave the museum.

同一幅作品可归档全景、局部与展牌等多张影像，题名、作者、年代与地点一并留存。展览结束很久之后，仍能快速找回当时站在画前的记忆。

Straighten the frame before you leave

Shoot from an angle and let the app correct perspective on your device. Choose a collection before you capture, and flip between photo and video when a piece deserves more than a single still.

斜对着画作拍摄也能在设备端拉正透视。开拍前选好要归入的作品集，照片与视频模式随时切换，动态装置或现场氛围也能完整留下。

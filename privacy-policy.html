GalleryLogic Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026 · App: GalleryLogic (com.artshot.app)

GalleryLogic is a museum and gallery photography app. This policy describes what information the app handles and how we use it. We designed GalleryLogic to work primarily on your device; we do not operate user accounts or sell your data.

Summary

  • Your photos, videos, and catalog metadata are stored on your device.
  • We do not run advertising or behavioral analytics in the app.
  • We do not upload your gallery to our servers (no cloud gallery in the current version).
  • Optional features (location, photo library, microphone) only run if you allow them.

Information you provide or create

  • Photos and videos you capture in the app, including originals and perspective-corrected copies, stored in the app’s private storage on your device.
  • Catalog metadata you enter or that the app suggests (for example title, artist, museum, exhibition, notes), stored locally in an on-device database.
  • Imported media you choose from your photo library, processed and stored like captured content.
  • App settings (for example correction mode, language, onboarding state), stored locally on your device.

Device permissions (optional except camera)

GalleryLogic requests system permissions only to provide features you use:

  • Camera (required) — to photograph artworks and labels. Images are processed on your device.
  • Photo library (optional) — to save corrected images to your system album when you enable album sync, and to import existing photos or videos you select.
  • Microphone (optional) — to record audio with videos. If denied, video recording may proceed without sound.
  • Location while using the app (optional) — if you opt in during setup, the app may read your approximate position after you capture media to suggest a museum or gallery name and address for metadata, and may store latitude and longitude with the artwork record on your device. Location is not tracked in the background.

You can change or revoke permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

On-device processing

To deliver core features, GalleryLogic processes your images on your device, including:

  • detecting artwork frames and applying perspective correction;
  • reading text from label photos (OCR) to suggest metadata fields;
  • classifying photo types (for example artwork vs. label).

This processing does not send your photos to GalleryLogic servers. It uses native frameworks on your phone (for example Apple Vision on iOS).

Location and geocoding

When location access is granted, coordinates may be sent to your platform’s geocoding service (Apple on iOS) to resolve a place name and address. That lookup is performed by the operating system provider under its own privacy terms; we receive only the result used to fill local metadata.

Purchases

If you buy in-app products (for example a Pro upgrade), payment is processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details. The app stores purchase entitlement status locally on your device and may verify receipts through Apple’s services as required for the purchase.

Feedback

If you contact us by email from the app, your mail app sends the message you write. The app may pre-fill non-personal diagnostic lines (app version, platform, device model, language) to help support. We use that correspondence only to respond to you.

What we do not collect

  • No GalleryLogic account or registration.
  • No sale of personal information.
  • No third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs in the current app.
  • No upload of your photo library to GalleryLogic-operated servers in the current version.

Retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or remove the app. Uninstalling GalleryLogic removes app-private storage; copies you saved to the system photo library remain under your control there.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. We will post the new version at this URL and update the effective date above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the support email shown in the App Store listing or in the app’s feedback settings.

GalleryLogic 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年6月5日 · 应用：GalleryLogic（com.artshot.app）

GalleryLogic 是一款博物馆与美术馆摄影应用。本政策说明应用如何处理您的信息。GalleryLogic 以本地使用为主，我们不提供用户账号，也不出售您的数据。

概要

  • 您拍摄的照片、视频及目录元数据保存在您的设备上。
  • 应用内无广告，也无行为分析/追踪。
  • 当前版本不会将您的图库上传至我们的服务器。
  • 位置、相册、麦克风等均为可选，仅在您授权后使用。

您创建或提供的信息

  • 照片与视频：含原图与透视校正结果，保存在应用私有目录。
  • 作品元数据：您填写或应用建议的标题、作者、美术馆、展览、备注等，保存在设备本地数据库。
  • 导入的媒体：您从系统相册选择的内容，按同样方式本地处理与存储。
  • 应用设置：如校正模式、语言、引导完成状态等，保存在设备本地。

系统权限

  • 相机（必需）：拍摄作品与展签，图像在设备上处理。
  • 相册（可选）：将校正后的图片保存到系统相册，或导入您选择的已有媒体。
  • 麦克风（可选）：为视频录制声音；拒绝时仍可无声录像。
  • 使用期间的位置（可选）：若在引导页开启，拍摄后可能读取大致位置以建议美术馆名称与地址，并可能在本地保存经纬度；不在后台持续定位。

设备端处理

包括画框检测、透视校正、展签文字识别（OCR）与照片类型判断等，均在您的设备上完成，不会将照片上传至 GalleryLogic 服务器（iOS 上使用系统等原生能力）。

定位与地理编码

授权位置后，坐标可能交由系统地理编码服务（iOS 上为 Apple）解析地名与地址；该服务适用其自身隐私条款，我们仅将结果用于填写本地元数据。

应用内购买

付款由 Apple 处理，我们不收集银行卡信息；购买权益状态保存在您的设备本地。

反馈邮件

通过应用发邮件时，内容由您的邮件客户端发送；应用可能预填应用版本、系统、设备型号、语言等非敏感信息以便支持。

我们不会

  • 不要求注册 GalleryLogic 账号；
  • 不出售个人信息；
  • 当前版本不接入广告或跨应用追踪 SDK；
  • 当前版本不上传您的图库至 GalleryLogic 服务器。

保留与删除

数据保留在您的设备上，直至您在应用内删除或卸载应用。卸载会清除应用私有数据；已保存到系统相册的副本仍由您自行管理。

政策变更

应用功能变更时我们可能更新本政策，并在此页面更新生效日期。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请通过 App Store 页面或应用内反馈中所列的支持邮箱联系我们。
